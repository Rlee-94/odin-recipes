Simple Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a great homemade lasagna recipe that takes some time and practice, but it's worth it!

Keep reading below as I show you the steps to creating this perfect Italian dish in under 2 hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (28oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 1/2 teapsoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 1/8 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 pound small curd cottage cheese
  • 3 eggs
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes, drain and set aside.
  2. Place pork and beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown. Stir in tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, parsley, garlic, oregano, onion, sugar, basil and salt. Simmer over medium-low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, place 2 layers of noodles on the bottom of dish; layer 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese and 1/2 of the sauce; repeat layers.
  4. Cover with aluminum foil and bake in preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 5 to 10 minutes; let stand for 10 minutes before cutting; serve.

Buon appetitio!

Enjoy your easy to make Lasagna. Don't forget the garlic bread!
